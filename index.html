Prayer Times In Muslim communities, people are reminded of the salat by the daily calls to prayer, known as adhan. The adhan is delivered from mosques by a muezzin, the mosque's designated caller of prayer. During the call to prayer, the muezzin recites the Takbir and the Kalimah.

The five prayers are called,

    1. Fajr
    2. Suhr
    3. \Asr
    4. Magrib
    5. Ishah
